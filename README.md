### 🧭 The short version

I'm a builder based in Singapore. I like taking ideas that are *almost* impossible to do well at small scale — elite chess coaching, proper fingerstyle guitar tuition, a blog that LLMs can actually read — and shipping them as real products.

Most of what I build is private (it pays the bills 😄), but the philosophy is open: **make hard things accessible, and build them right from the start.**
